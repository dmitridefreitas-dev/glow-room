The Glow Room · Product Walkthrough

How a member moves through the product

A step-by-step map of the full journey — from landing on the site to checking in every day — and exactly what each person can access at each stage. Read it top to bottom; access widens as they go.

Prepared for the business owner · reflects the live build (Next.js app + Stripe + Discord) · June 2026

The four access tiers (who can do what)

Tier 0
Visitor
Anyone on the internet. Sees the marketing site only. No login.
Tier 1
Account holder
Signed up but hasn't paid. Can reach the join/checkout page — nothing else.
Tier 2
Paying member
Enrolled in a cohort. Full app: daily check-ins, streak, leaderboard, badges.
Tier 3
Community member
Verified in Discord with the Member role. Access to the members-only community.

The journey, step by step

1

Discover the Glow Room

Public /

The marketing landing page. Explains the promise — a 30-day glow up you actually finish, together — and the four pillars (Movement, Skin, Mindset, Habit Anchor). Shows the cohort start dates (Jan 1 · Apr 1 · Jul 1 · Oct 1). Two buttons, both lead to login: “Enter the Glow Room” and “Sign in.”

⬚ What they can access here
Marketing & pillar info Cohort schedule Everything else is locked
Clicks “Enter the Glow Room”
2

Create an account (or sign in)

Public /login

Email + password — no waiting on a confirmation email. New accounts are created already-confirmed, so they can sign in immediately. “Continue with Google” is available too. This is where a visitor becomes a Tier 1 account holder.

4

Pay on Stripe

Action /api/checkout → Stripe

The app hands off to Stripe’s secure hosted checkout — the card form lives on Stripe, never on our site. The session quietly carries who they are and which cohort, so we can finish the job after payment. On success Stripe sends them to the welcome page.

⬚ State after paying
Payment captured by Stripe Provisioning kicks off automatically →
Stripe notifies our server (webhook)
5

Account is provisioned automatically

Behind the scenes /api/webhooks/stripe

The member does nothing here — this all happens server-side the moment payment confirms. On checkout.session.completed the system:

Creates the cohort enrollment Generates a single-use access code Emails the code (Resend) Membership → records the subscription
Built to be safe: it’s idempotent (won’t double-enroll if Stripe retries), and a refund or chargeback later automatically revokes access and removes the Discord role.
Member lands on the welcome screen
6

“You’re in!” — get the code

Secure page /welcome

Confirms payment and shows their single-use Discord code with instructions: join Discord, go to #verify, run /verify code:GLW-…. They’re now a Tier 2 paying member — the dashboard is unlocked.

⬚ Newly unlocked
Full dashboard access Their Discord verify code Community still needs the verify step →
Takes the code into Discord
7

Verify in the Discord community

Discord /verify · bot → /api/discord/redeem

In the server’s #verify channel they run the slash command with their code. The bot checks it against our API, burns the code so it can’t be reused, links their Discord account to their membership, and grants the Member role. This is the step that just promoted you to Server Member. ✅

⬚ Newly unlocked — now Tier 3
Member-only channels The cohort community Code is now spent (one use only)
First visit to the dashboard
8

One-time setup: pick a habit anchor

Secure page Gate /dashboard/intake

Before Day 1, the dashboard makes them choose their habit anchor — one small non-negotiable they’ll repeat every day (make my bed, 2 L water, read 10 pages, go outside, or their own). It’s the metronome the whole challenge plays against. They can’t reach the dashboard until this is set; once chosen, it’s skipped forever.

Anchor locked in — enters the daily loop
9

The daily loop — the core product

Secure page /dashboard

Home base for a paying member. Shows their streak, days complete, today’s day number, a progress bar, and a 30-day grid. Everything a member uses day to day lives here:

⬚ Full member access
Daily check-in · /dashboard/day/[n] Leaderboard · /dashboard/leaderboard Collectible badges 30-day progress grid Manage billing · /api/portal
↳ Inside a daily check-in

Tick the four pillars — Movement, Skin, Mindset, Habit Anchor — add optional notes, and upload a private progress photo. A day only counts as “complete” when all of its pillars are done. Completing days builds the streak and badges.

Anti-cheat by design: days unlock one at a time at midnight — you can’t pre-fill the whole challenge to jump the leaderboard. This is enforced in the database, so it holds even against someone poking the API directly. The leaderboard only ever exposes a name and counts.

Milestones that keep them coming back

★ First Day ★ 7-Day Streak ★ Halfway (Day 15) ★ Challenge Complete Auto-awarded as they progress — a collector mechanic that rewards consistency.

What happens after — keeping access honest

Access can also be taken away — automatically

The same webhook that grants access also protects the business if a payment is reversed or a subscription ends. No manual cleanup required.

Refund or chargeback
The cohort enrollment is revoked and the Discord Member role is removed — they lose app and community access.
Membership cancelled
When the subscription ends in Stripe, the subscription is marked cancelled and the Discord role is removed.
